Climbr - Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

At Climbr, we respect and protect your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use our app.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: username, email, and password
  • Climbing activity data: routes, difficulty, attempts, and progress
  • User-generated content: photos, posts, and comments
  • Location of climbing gyms (when you identify them)

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our services
  • To enable you to record and track your climbing sessions
  • To maintain a community of climbers

Data Security

Your data is stored on secure servers and transmitted using encryption (HTTPS). We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your personal information.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You can delete your account at any time through the app or by requesting it using the form below.
